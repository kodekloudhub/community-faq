# KKE and 100 Days Challenges

This mini FAQ pertains to the task unlock logic for KodeKloud Engineer (KKE) tasks. 100 Days challenges are part of the KKE platform therefore there are some similarities.

## Task Unlock Logic

The following describes in what order and how frequently you can attempt the tasks, which is dependent on your subscription plan.

### Free and Standard Plans

The general rule across the entire KKE platform is **1 task per day**, irrespective of whether the task you do is a 100 days or a regular task. A day from the point of view of this platform begins at 00:00 UTC (05:30 IST).

**Unlock Logic**
* When a topic is added to **Active Practice, 1 task unlocks per day**
* 100-days tasks unlock **strictly in sequence**
* Next task unlocks **only after the previous task is marked as completed**

**Attempt Restrictions**
* You can attempt **only 1 task per day**
* Even if the next task is unlocked, you must **wait until the next day** to attempt it
* **No skipping** of 100-days tasks allowed

### Pro and AI Plans

On these plans there are no restrictions to the *number* of tasks you can attempt in one day, so you can do some 100-days or some KKE tasks or a combination of both.

**Unlock Logic**
* When a topic is added to **Active Practice**, you can unlock **multiple tasks on the same day**
* 100-days tasks unlock **strictly in sequence**
* Next task unlocks **only after the previous task is marked as completed**

**Attempt Restrictions**
* You can attempt **unlimited tasks per day**
* **No skipping** of 100-days tasks allowed
