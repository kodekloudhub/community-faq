# Exam vouchers

KodeKloud offers a scheme whereby students can purchase vouchers for the CKA exam at a 55% discount over the standard Linux Foundation cost. However this has a few important rules

* The offer is available only to students resident in India.
* You obtain the voucher from KodeKloud and use it at the Linux Foundation checkout.
* The free retake is not displayed at the Linux Foundation checkout. This is expected. Should you not pass on the first attempt, reach out to KodeKloud with your result to obtain a retake voucher.
* The killer.sh simulator is *not* included when purchasing the CKA exam voucher through KodeKloud. This is due to the arrangement we have with Linux Foundation. We are confident that the material in the course along with the [Ultimate Mock Exam](https://learn.kodekloud.com/courses/ultimate-certified-kubernetes-administrator-cka-mock-exam-series) series is sufficient to pass. Should you still wish to do Killer, you can purchase directly from their [website](https://killer.sh/) for 39.99 USD.

Students in other countries should monitor the Linux Foundation website for offers which do happen several times a year (and always on Cyber Weekend in November) offering variously discounts between 30 and 45%. Offers directly from LF include everything that is part of the standard exam package.
